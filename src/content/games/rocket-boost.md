Rocket Boost is a challenging physics-based game built in Unity. Navigate your rocket through treacherous obstacle courses using precise thrust control. Each level introduces new hazards and tighter corridors that will test your reflexes and patience.

## Controls

- **Spacebar** — Main thrust
- **A / Left Arrow** — Rotate left
- **D / Right Arrow** — Rotate right

## Tips

- Short bursts of thrust give you more control than holding it down
- Watch your rotation — a tilted rocket is hard to recover
- Crashing resets you to the start of the level, so take it slow!
